About MacFixer

Trusted by thousands of Mac users since 2019, we turn frustrating macOS problems into smooth, reliable performance.

Our Mission

MacFixer was born from a simple frustration: too many Mac users waste hours searching forums, running untested Terminal commands, and hoping for the best. We believe professional-grade troubleshooting should be accessible to everyone — not just IT departments with six-figure budgets.

Our mission is to deliver fast, transparent, and data-safe macOS repairs at a fair price. Every fix comes with a plain-language explanation of what went wrong and how to prevent it from happening again.

Since our founding in San Francisco, we have resolved over 28,000 support tickets spanning every macOS release from High Sierra to the latest Sequoia, across MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, iMac, Mac Mini, Mac Studio, and Mac Pro hardware.

Our Core Values

🔎

Root-Cause Thinking

We never patch symptoms. Every repair starts with identifying the true underlying cause — whether it is a corrupted preference file, a rogue kernel extension, or a failing SSD sector.

🔒

Data Integrity First

Before we touch your system we create verified backups. Our technicians follow a strict chain-of-custody protocol so your documents, photos, and credentials are never at risk.

💬

Honest Communication

No jargon walls. We explain every diagnosis in plain language, share our screen during remote sessions, and provide a written summary you can refer to later.

Ready to Fix Your Mac?

Whether it is a one-time bug or an ongoing fleet issue, our team is standing by to help you reclaim peak performance.
