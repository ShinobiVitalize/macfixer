Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to site owners. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until they expire or you delete them) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser).

2. How MacFixer Uses Cookies

When you visit macfixer.com, we may place cookies on your device to help us understand how you interact with our site, remember your preferences, and improve our services. We categorize our cookies into three types:

3. Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of our website. They enable core features such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly. Necessary cookies do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled.

  • cookieConsent — Stores your cookie consent preference so we do not show the banner repeatedly. Duration: 1 year.
  • session_id — Maintains your session state during a visit. Duration: session.

4. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow our website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we use on our pages.

  • preferred_service — Remembers the service tier you last selected in our contact form. Duration: 30 days.
  • font_size — Stores your preferred text size if you use accessibility controls. Duration: 1 year.

5. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps us improve the structure and content of our site.

  • _ga / _gid — Google Analytics cookies that distinguish unique users and track page views. Duration: _ga expires after 2 years; _gid expires after 24 hours.
  • _gat — Used by Google Analytics to throttle request rate. Duration: 1 minute.

We configure Google Analytics to anonymize IP addresses and do not use it for advertising or cross-site tracking.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Third parties that may set cookies through our site include:

  • Google Analytics — For aggregated website usage analysis.
  • Google Fonts — For loading the Inter typeface used on our site.
  • Stripe — For processing secure payments when you purchase a service plan.

Each third party operates under its own privacy and cookie policies.

7. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings. Visit your browser's help documentation for instructions specific to your browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
  • Our Cookie Banner: When you first visit our site, a banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.
  • Opt-Out Links: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and limit your ability to use some features.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
