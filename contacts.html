Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most remote diagnostic sessions are completed within 30 to 90 minutes depending on complexity. If we discover a hardware-level problem that requires physical inspection, we will schedule an on-site visit within 24 hours for Bay Area clients or arrange a secure mail-in process for others.

Will I lose any data during the repair?

No. Before any system-level changes, we create a verified Time Machine or Carbon Copy Cloner backup. If a backup is not possible due to a failing drive, we will discuss recovery options with you before proceeding.

Do you support older Mac models?

Yes. We support every Mac model from 2012 onward and every macOS release from High Sierra (10.13) through the latest Sequoia. If your machine is older, contact us and we will let you know what options are available.
